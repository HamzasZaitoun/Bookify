Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bookify?

Bookify is an online bookstore where you can explore, purchase, and review a variety of books across different genres.

How long does it take to deliver a book?

Typically, delivery takes 3-5 business days, depending on your location.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods for your convenience.

Can I return a book?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for books in their original condition. Please refer to our return policy for more details.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email at support@bookify.com or through our live chat on the website.
